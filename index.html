§ 01 · The Book

The Metaphysics of the Made.

Author
W. Clark Wolf
Publisher
CUP, 2026

Wolf argues that Hegel's mature project is an inversion of the philosophical tradition. In the Science of Logic, Hegel works out the metaphysical basis for a philosophy where the paradigm of knowledge is thought's knowledge of its own products. Geist, not nature, sets the standard.
